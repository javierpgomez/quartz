# Steps in Transplanting

## Source:
- [[The New Gardeners handbook]]
by [[Daryl Beyers]]

## Key Ideas
1. Remove the plant from its pot
2. Check the roots
3. Dig a hole
4. Position the plant
5. Backfill with soil
6. Make a raised bank to direct the water
7. Mulch
