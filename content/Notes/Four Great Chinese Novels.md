### Key Idea
The four great Chinese novels are:

-   Monkey King (Journey to the West) by Wu Cheng’en
-   Romance of the Three Kingdoms by Luo Guanzhong
-   Water Margin by Shi Nai’an
-   Dream of the Red Chamber by Cao Xueqin
